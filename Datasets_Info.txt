1. Original Dataset Source (Kaggle)
    Title : New Plant Diseases Dataset

    Provided by :   Vipoooool

    Link :  https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vipoooool/new-plant-diseases-dataset

    Description:    This dataset contains RGB images of healthy and diseased crop leaves. It is categorized into 38 classes and is commonly used for plant disease detection using deep learning. The dataset is structured into training, validation, and test sets.
